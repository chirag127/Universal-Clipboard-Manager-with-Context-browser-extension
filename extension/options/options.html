Privacy Policy

ClipContext Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2024

1. Information We Collect

ClipContext collects the following information:

  • Content you copy to your clipboard while browsing
  • The URL and title of the webpage where the copy action occurred
  • Timestamps of when items were copied

2. How We Use Your Information

We use this information solely to provide the clipboard history functionality of the extension. Specifically:

  • To display your clipboard history in the extension popup
  • To categorize content using AI for better organization
  • To enable searching and filtering of your clipboard history

3. Data Storage

Your clipboard data is stored:

  • On our servers, associated with an anonymous user ID
  • This ID is randomly generated and not linked to any personally identifiable information

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your clipboard data with any third parties except:

  • When using the AI categorization feature, text content is sent to Google's Gemini AI API for categorization purposes only

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Disable clipboard monitoring at any time
  • Clear your clipboard history
  • Export your data
  • Disable AI categorization

6. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your clipboard data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy in the extension.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at [contact email].
